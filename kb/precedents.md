## What counts as a precedent

A precedent is a past decision or outcome that:
* Was deliberately made and documented.
* Is expected to recur or influence future decisions.
* Has been validated through repeated success or formal acceptance.

## Precedent promotion rule

A pattern becomes a precedent when:
1. It has succeeded in at least two separate executions.
2. It has been explicitly reviewed and accepted.
3. It is documented here with a source reference.
